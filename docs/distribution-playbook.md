# UKM Distribution Playbook

## Primary channels
- `WhatsApp`: use short, direct copy with the link at the end.
- `Instagram Story`: use the tall story card with the prompt as the visual headline.
- `Generic share sheet`: use the emotional variant when you need curiosity, and the short variant when you need clarity.

## Launch prompts to test
- `Ask me anything`
- `Be honest about me`
- `What do you think of me?`
- `Tell me something I should know`

## Manual copy testing rule
- Test 5-10 prompt/caption pairs before changing product logic.
- Keep only the top 2 by share-to-submit conversion.
- Do not automate ranking until the cohort thresholds in launch mode are met.

## Posting rhythm
- Day 1: one share at setup, one share 4-6 hours later with a different copy variant.
- Day 2: if there are no organic messages, switch prompt and share again.
- Day 3+: reuse only the prompt/copy pairs that are already producing replies.

## Creative constraints
- Never fake sender identity.
- Never imply you can reveal who sent a message.
- Keep the CTA short: prompt first, link second.
